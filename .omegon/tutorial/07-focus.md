---
title: "Focus Mode"
---

Try typing **/focus** in the input box and pressing Enter.

The instrument panel disappears — the conversation area expands to fill the full height. This is focus mode: maximum screen space for reading and writing, no distractions.

Type **/focus** again to bring the instruments back.

This toggle is useful when you're deep in a conversation and don't need the telemetry, or when you want to maximize the visible conversation history.

Type **/next** when ready for the final lesson.
