title: "The Cockpit"
---

Welcome to Omegon! Let's start by understanding what you're looking at.

Look at the **bottom of your screen**. You'll see two main areas:

**Engine Panel (bottom-left):** This shows the current model name, provider, tier (Victory/Gloriana/Retribution), thinking level, and context percentage. This is your at-a-glance status of the inference engine.

**Instrument Panel (bottom-right):** This has two sub-panels:
- **Left — Inference:** A gradient bar showing context usage (navy = empty, teal = healthy, amber = hot). When I'm thinking, glitch characters appear on this bar. Below it, tree-connected "memory strings" show fact counts per linked mind.
- **Right — Tools:** A sorted list of tools I've called, ordered by most recent. Each shows a recency bar and time since last use.

Take a moment to look at these areas. The engine panel should show the model I'm running on. The instrument panel might be mostly dark right now — we haven't done much yet.

That's the cockpit. When you're ready to see it light up, type **/next**.
